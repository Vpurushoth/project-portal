ABOUT US

VP INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

VPIET has become the crown jewel in the field of technical education since its inception. The institution is governed by visionary extraordinaire philanthropist Shri. V. Purushothaman, whose contribution to the Indian Jewel Industry and Tourism and Hotel Industry remains outstanding.

Approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, the institution has dawned as a present day doyen of Engineering Education.
